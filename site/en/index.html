FAQ

Before you get in touch.

Can I rent the studio without virtual production experience?

Yes. In the “studio + operator” model our team runs the LED wall, tracking and Unreal Engine – your team focuses on camera and content.

What does a virtual production cost?

Entry-level projects such as the recruiting day start in the low five figures – with locations, travel and shoot days saved, the total budget is often below that of a classic shoot.

Can you tell it isn't real?

With correct tracking and lighting: no. The environment reacts in perspective-correct fashion to every camera move – unlike greenscreen, you see the finished image on set.

Do you work with English-speaking crews?

Yes. We work in English with international brands and agencies, and act as your production partner and fixer in Germany.

Can we use our CAD / 3D data?

Yes. Product and plant data (CAD, BIM, 3D models) are prepared for Unreal Engine and turned into a walkable film set.
