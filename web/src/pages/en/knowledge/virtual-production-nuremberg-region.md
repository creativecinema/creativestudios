The Nuremberg metropolitan region is one of Europe's strongest industrial areas: machinery, medical technology, IT and a dense base of mid-sized companies from Erlangen through Fürth to Nuremberg. What many of them lack is high-end moving image — and a short route to producing it. This is where **virtual production on your doorstep** becomes an underrated advantage.

## A film studio in the region — instead of travelling to Berlin or Munich

A demanding brand film or product clip used to mean travelling to a big studio city: crew, gear and decision-makers on the road for days, plus travel and hotel costs. Our **LED studio in Erlangen** sits 20 minutes from Nuremberg, with direct access to the A3 and A73 motorways. For companies in and around Nuremberg, Erlangen and Fürth — and for international productions looking for a base in southern Germany — that means: arrive, shoot, done the same day.

## What companies shoot with virtual production

The LED wall renders photorealistic environments in real time — the set exists during the shoot, not weeks later in post. Typical regional projects:

- **Product & technology films:** machines, devices or medical technology against changing, on-brand backdrops — clean room, factory floor, abstract brand world.
- **Trade-fair & sales videos:** several looks in one shoot day, tailored to different markets.
- **Recruiting & brand films:** a modern, high-end look that fits a tech location.
- **Training & explainer content:** recurring settings, plannable and reproducible.

The key: **up to five "locations" in a single shoot day**, weather-independent and without filming permits.

## Why local is a genuine advantage

- **Short distances, sign-off on set.** Your marketing lead or management drops by for an hour, sees the finished image in camera and approves on the spot — instead of week-long review loops.
- **Predictable cost.** No travel days, no location logistics, less post. A shoot week often becomes a shoot day. Our [cost calculator](/wissen/kosten-drehtag/) shows what that means in practice.
- **Regional value.** Contacts and crew from the region, fast scheduling, short communication paths.
